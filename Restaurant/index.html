A Legacy of Flavour, Spices & Tradition

Founded in 2012, {{CLINIC_NAME}} has been serving authentic Rajasthani and North Indian delicacies to the food lovers of Jodhpur for over 14 years. What started as a modest local dhaba has grown into a beloved fine casual dining destination, keeping our roots firmly anchored in traditional culinary craft.

Our secret lies in our adherence to ancient cooking methods: hand-grinding local Mathania chilies, slow-cooking in copper handis, and baking breads in traditional clay tandoors. Every recipe has been passed down through our family, guaranteeing an aromatic trip down memory lane with every single bite.
