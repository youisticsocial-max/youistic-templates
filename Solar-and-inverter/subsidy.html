Slabs & Documentation Checklists

The PM Surya Ghar scheme releases direct benefits to the bank account of empanelled agency customers upon successfully commissioning the net meter.

Required Documents:

  • ✔ Active JDVVNL Electricity Account Connection Number
  • ✔ Copy of Recent Electricity Bill (Matching installation address)
  • ✔ Aadhaar Card of the Connection Owner
  • ✔ Proof of Roof Ownership / NOC if rental site
  • ✔ Active Bank Account Passbook (For direct subsidy credit)
