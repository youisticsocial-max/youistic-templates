How We Work

Home / How We Work
Workflow Policy

Engineered For Stress-Free Transitions

Clear milestones that dictate how we handle packaging safety, customs clearances, loading, and transit protection.

1

Site Survey & Material Assessment

Our logistics executive visits your site (or schedules a video survey) to evaluate the volume of goods, count fragile items, check lift heights, and plan optimal carton sizes.

2

Premium Packaging Deployment

On shifting day, our crew arrives with bubble wraps, corrugated sheets, foam rolls, and heavy boxes. Items are individually cataloged, packaged, and labeled for tracking.

3

Stacking & Weight Distribution Loading

Heavier items go at the base of the container, securely tied with cargo straps to avoid sliding. Fragile packages sit at the top. The load is locked and sealed under supervision.

4

Real-time GPS Monitored Transit

The fleet moves along optimized corridors. Clients can check shipment locations dynamically on our website tracker using their assigned tracking ID.
